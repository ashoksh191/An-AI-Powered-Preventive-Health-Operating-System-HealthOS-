HealthOS

The AI-Powered Preventive Health Operating System

Presented by: Team BioSynth

Redefining Healthcare from Reactive Treatment to Preventive Longevity

The Problem Statement

The Reactive Healthcare Epidemic

✕ Reactive Treatments
Modern healthcare intervenes only after disease onset. This reactive approach is highly expensive, stressful, and results in poorer outcomes.
✕ One-Size-Fits-All Nutrition
Standard diet trackers assume expensive, hard-to-source ingredients and ignore local regional cuisine, budgets, and prep-time restrictions.
✕ Wearable & Data Silos
Health statistics, blood panels, and radiology results remain isolated in paperwork, missing critical early-stage preventative correlations.
✕ Lack of Local Language Accessibility
Advanced health coaching algorithms are primarily locked behind high cost walls and english-only interfaces, ignoring non-english cohorts.

The Solution

Introducing HealthOS Ecosystem

✓ Constraint-Aware Protocols
AI generates 1-day personalized health protocols that adapt strictly to local cuisine budgets, prep times, and home gym equipment.
✓ Integrated Diagnostics
Physician Co-pilot, Blood Biomarker Extraction, and Radiology AI Summarization working in one unified, collaborative dashboard.
✓ Multi-Language Voice Assistant
Hands-free voice recognition & text-to-speech assistant operating in 12 regional languages (including Hinglish and Hindi).

AI & Machine Learning Capabilities

High-Performance Integration of Gemini AI

  • AI Longevity Clock Engine: Clinically grounded calculations mapping BMI, exercise, sleep, and lifestyle to output Biological Age vs Chronological Age.
  • Vision AI Meal Analyzer: Gemini Vision API scans food photos to extract calories, protein, carbs, fats, and rate clinical suitability.
  • Lab Report Clinical Biomarker Analyzer: Auto-extracts HbA1c, lipids, TSH, creatinine levels, flagging out-of-range clinical anomalies in color-coded badges.
  • Differential Diagnosis Copilot: Real-time clinical recommendations and differential lists for medical practitioners.
